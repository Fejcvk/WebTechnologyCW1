Cultural city center | GLIWICE

The main goal of the project was to create not only an architectural building, but also a small public open space. By drawing up two opposite corners a pedestrian route was created between the city center and the main city park. It makes our object an important part of the walking route and will encourage citizens to visit the cultural center. The facade of the building is covered with frosted glass which when illuminated focuses the attention of the pede- strians. The inner facade is covered with clear glass to create a special relationship between the interior and exterior.

  • Location: Gliwice | Poland
