Mixed - use development

The main idea was to create a building that connects the shopping and housing zone in an opti- mal way. The aim was to move away from conventional multifamily housing division, where on the ground floor or on the first two floors there are service areas. By splitting the object along a diagonal line, the shopping zone has become more spacious without detriment to the housing zone. To separate the two zones i added an extra floor between them, which was filled by recreation areas. In this way i improved the quality of life of the residents and created the floor which acts as a buffer.

  • Type : Mixed - use development
  • Location: Wodzislaw Slaski | Poland
