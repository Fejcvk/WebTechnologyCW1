Poprzedni Następny

Kids cafe | Katowice

My goal was to design a small interactive center for kids with an additional function of a café. I created space as colorful as the area surrounding cafe. The kitchen is yellow, kindergarten is blue, café is red, and the interactive area lacks color – the technology brings the colors there. Space has continuity but constantly changing. In this case modern, an interactive area is next to cozy, peaceful café. There are no walls – spaces lay next to each other but remain very different.

  • Type : Cafe and kids space
  • Location: Katowice | Poland
