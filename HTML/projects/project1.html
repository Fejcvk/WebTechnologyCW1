Science Center „Rymer” | RYBNIK

To develop the function of the existing Center of culture, we decided to create an object by expanding its scope. Objects such as the Science Center attract many tourists as well as local residents. Its impact does not depend on the attractiveness of the city and surrounding area. I aimed to create an object which did not interfere with the park and nearby existing houses. Referring to the topography of the place, the landfill becomes an advantage and the symbol of the area. Maintaining existing pedestrian paths had an impact on how the building would be shared. The center is immersed in greenery. The southern areas received a new recreation function. The landfill after the completion of the process of reclamation it would become an area for walking.
